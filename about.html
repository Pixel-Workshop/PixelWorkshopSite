Our Story

Born out of frustration
with bloated software.

It started with a simple observation: most apps do too much, ask for too much, and respect you too little. Endless subscription prompts, invasive ads, feature bloat that buries the one thing you actually opened the app to do.

So we started building our own. Retro Handheld came first ‚Äî a Game Boy emulator that just works, no strings attached. Then Solve To Snooze, because alarm clocks have been boring for too long and we needed something that actually got us out of bed.

How We Work

Small team.
AI-powered velocity.

We use AI throughout our development process ‚Äî not as a shortcut, but as a force multiplier. It lets us move fast without cutting corners on quality.

01

Start with a real problem

Every app starts with something that genuinely annoyed us, or a gap we noticed in the market. If we wouldn't use it ourselves every day, we don't build it.

02

Design for clarity, not features

We resist the urge to add. Every feature has to justify its existence. If something doesn't make the core experience better, it doesn't make it into the app.

03

Build fast with AI assistance

We integrate AI at every stage ‚Äî prototyping, code generation, QA, and copy. This lets our small team ship with the output of a much larger one, without ballooning headcount or timelines.

04

Ship, listen, improve

We ship when something is genuinely good ‚Äî not when it hits an arbitrary deadline. After launch, we listen to users and iterate quickly. No two-year roadmaps, no feature freezes.
