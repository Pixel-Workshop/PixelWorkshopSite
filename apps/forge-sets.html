Approach

Programming should stay flexible.

ForgeSets is for lifters who care about the work itself. A good strength planner should make the plan clearer, not bury it under social feeds, badges, or dashboards that do not help you train.

Training changes in real life. You miss a day, swap an exercise, or adjust volume mid-block. ForgeSets is built to absorb those changes without turning your whole program into admin work, and AI is there to help you adjust the plan faster while keeping the structure visible.
