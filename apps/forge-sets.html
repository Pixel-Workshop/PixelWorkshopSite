Features

Programming That Holds Up

🏋️

Structured Programs

Build lifting plans around upper/lower splits, full-body templates, or your own weekly structure.

📈

Progressive Overload

Track weight, reps, and target progression so each training block has a clear path forward.

🗓️

Day-by-Day Planning

See each workout laid out in order with prescribed sets, rep ranges, and notes before you start.

⏱️

Rest and Session Flow

Move through workouts without second-guessing timing or losing track of where you are in the session.

🧱

Blocks and Cycles

Organize training into phases so intensity, volume, and recovery make sense over time.

📶

Offline First

Your plan stays available in the gym, on the road, or anywhere else you train.

Approach

Less noise. Better training.

ForgeSets is for people who care about the work itself. The app should make your programming clearer, not bury it under social feeds, badges, or inflated dashboards.

You set the plan, show up, and lift. ForgeSets keeps the structure tight so your attention stays on execution and progress rather than busywork.
