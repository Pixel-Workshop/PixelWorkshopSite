Solve To Snooze

No more mindlessly hitting snooze. Solve a quick math puzzle to dismiss an alarm, build better mornings, and stay accountable to your daily routine. Many people also use Solve To Snooze for important reminders like medication, where a simple tap can be too easy to ignore.

Features

Wake Up Smarter

🧮

Math-Gated Snooze

Earn your snooze by solving a math problem. No cheating. No correct answer, no snooze.

🎯

Adjustable Difficulty

Choose easy, medium, or hard problems depending on how much of a challenge you want.

⏰

Multiple Alarms

Set as many alarms as you need, each with its own schedule, label, and difficulty level.

💊

Medication & Routine Reminders

Use alarms as reminders for routines like taking medication, staying hydrated, or starting a planned task. The challenge helps prevent accidental dismissals.

🔔

Custom Sounds

Pick from built-in alarm tones to find one that wakes you up without ruining your morning.

📅

Repeating Schedules

Set alarms to repeat on specific days of the week. Perfect for weekday routines.

🔒

Private by Design

Everything stays on your device. No account needed, no data collected, no ads.
