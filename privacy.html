Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026  •  Applies to: Retro Handheld, Solve To Snooze

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Pixel Workshop LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information across all of our apps. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps do not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to us
  • Any behavioral or usage patterns

Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated by our apps — including settings, preferences, game saves, alarm schedules, and any other app data — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us at any time.

If you delete an app, all locally stored data associated with it will be removed from your device.

Notifications (Solve To Snooze)

Solve To Snooze requests permission to send local notifications in order to trigger alarms. These are local notifications scheduled on your device only — no information is transmitted when an alarm fires, and no external servers are involved.

You can revoke notification permissions at any time via iPhone Settings → Notifications → Solve To Snooze.

ROM Files (Retro Handheld)

Any ROM files you import into Retro Handheld are stored locally on your device. We have no access to these files at any time. You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the legal right to use any ROM files you import.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Our apps do not make any network requests during normal use.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by not collecting any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support page.
