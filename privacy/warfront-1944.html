Privacy Policy

Warfront: 1944

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Warfront: 1944 ("the App"), developed by Pixel Workshop LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), handles your information. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Warfront: 1944 does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to us
  • Any gameplay statistics or progress data

Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated by Warfront: 1944 — including your campaign progress, skirmish settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.

In-App Purchases

Warfront: 1944 may offer optional add-on content packs as in-app purchases. All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment information, including credit card numbers or billing details.

Third-Party Services

Warfront: 1944 does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not make any network requests beyond what is required by the App Store for in-app purchase verification.

Children's Privacy

Warfront: 1944 does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by not collecting any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support page.
