Frequently Asked Questions

Calmly supports Pomodoro-style sessions (25 min work / 5 min break), custom interval timers where you set your own durations, and open-ended sessions with no fixed end time. You can switch modes freely from the settings screen.

Calmly logs each completed session locally on your device. You can view your daily and weekly focus totals, as well as your current streak, from the History tab. All data stays on your device — nothing is sent to any server.
