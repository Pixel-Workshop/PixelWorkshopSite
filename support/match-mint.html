Frequently Asked Questions

Flip cards to reveal symbols, then find matching pairs before the round timer expires. The faster you clear pairs, the higher your score multiplier becomes.
Consecutive matches without mistakes increase your combo streak. A wrong flip resets the streak. Longer streaks earn bonus points and improve leaderboard placement.
Daily Challenge gives you one curated board each day with fixed rules. Completing it tracks your participation streak and helps you compare your performance over time.
