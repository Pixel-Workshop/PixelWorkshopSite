FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Warfront: 1944 is currently in development. We'll announce a release date when it's closer to launch. Use the contact form below to get in touch with any questions.
Warfront: 1944 is being built for iPhone and iPad. We'll confirm minimum iOS version requirements closer to launch.
The game features two modes: Campaign and Skirmish. Campaign takes you through a series of story-driven missions across WWII theaters. Skirmish lets you set up a custom battle against AI on any unlocked map with your choice of difficulty and starting resources.
Campaign progress is saved automatically after each mission completes. You can quit and return to the app without losing your place. All save data is stored locally on your device.
Yes. Completed campaign missions can be replayed from the mission select screen, so you can try for a better result or just enjoy the battle again.
Add-on packs are optional paid content that expand the game with new campaigns, maps, and units. Planned packs include Operation Overlord (D-Day), Pacific Theater, and Eastern Front. The base game is a complete experience without them.
Yes. Warfront: 1944 is designed to be fully playable offline. No internet connection is required for campaign or skirmish modes.
No. Warfront: 1944 does not collect personal data. All game data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
